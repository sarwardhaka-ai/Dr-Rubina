Dr. Mosammat Rubina Sultana is a distinguished Cancer Specialist and Radiation Oncologist in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with 22+ years of clinical experience and academic leadership. She holds MBBS, BCS (Health), FCPS (Radiotherapy), and is IAEA-certified in Radiotherapy & Brachytherapy. As an active member of the Bangladesh Society of Radiation Oncologists (BSRO) and the Oncology Club, Dr. Rubina combines global standards with local expertise. She currently serves as an Assistant Professor in the Radiation Oncology Department at the National Institute of Cancer Research & Hospital (NICRH).

Dr. Rubina Sultana specializes in treating cervical cancer, breast cancer, rectal cancer, lung cancer, brain tumors, soft tissue sarcoma, head and neck, gastrointestinal cancers, and metastatic cancers. Her clinical skills include radiotherapy, chemotherapy, brachytherapy, cancer diagnosis and staging, and supportive and palliative care. She is known for her compassionate, evidence-based treatment plans tailored to each patient's condition and stage.

Patients can consult Dr. Rubina Sultana at leading hospitals in Dhaka: Ahsania Mission Cancer & General Hospital (Uttara), Delta Hospital (Mirpur), and Enam Medical College & Hospital (Savar). Her commitment to patient-centered care, combined with deep expertise and ethical practice, makes her one of the most trusted oncologists in Bangladesh.
