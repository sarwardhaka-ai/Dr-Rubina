👩‍⚕️ About Dr. Rubina Sultana

Dr. Mosammat Rubina Sultana is a nationally recognized and highly respected leading cancer specialist in Bangladesh, renowned for her dual expertise in clinical oncology and radiation therapy. With over 22 years of experience, she has earned national recognition for delivering compassionate, evidence-based cancer care across all stages — from early diagnosis to advanced palliative support.

She currently serves as an Associate Professor in the Department of Radiation Oncology at the National Institute of Cancer Research & Hospital (NICRH), Dhaka — Bangladesh’s leading center for cancer research and advanced treatment. As an academic leader, she is committed to training and mentoring the next generation of oncologists, shaping the future of cancer treatment in Bangladesh.

In addition to her academic role, she is a Consultant at the modern and well-equipped Ahsania Mission Cancer & General Hospital — one of the country’s top private cancer hospitals — and a Visiting Consultant at both Delta Hospital Ltd., Mirpur-1 and Enam Medical College Hospital, Savar.

She remains at the forefront of radiation oncology advancements, utilizing modern techniques such as IMRT (Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy), IGRT (Image-Guided Radiation Therapy), and SBRT (Stereotactic Body Radiation Therapy). Each treatment plan is carefully tailored to the patient’s unique cancer type, stage, and overall health — ensuring precision, safety, and personalized care.

Dr. Rubina Sultana is an IAEA-certified radiation oncologist, having completed advanced international training in radiotherapy and brachytherapy through the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA). She holds an MBBS from Chittagong Medical College and an FCPS in Radiotherapy — one of the highest qualifications in cancer care. She began her career in public service through the BCS (Health) cadre.

Respected for her gentle communication style and clinical precision, she is deeply committed to empowering patients and families. She simplifies complex medical decisions, ensures informed understanding, and provides unwavering support throughout the treatment journey.

She contributes actively to the Bangladesh Oncology Journal and participates in national and international cancer initiatives. Her work reflects a rare combination of technical excellence, academic leadership, and compassionate care — making her one of the most trusted names in oncology in Bangladesh.

She is also highly rated and trusted by patients across verified health directories in Bangladesh and international platforms such as ClinicSpots (India). Her compassionate care fosters exceptional patient satisfaction, deep trust, and healing — earning her recognition as one of the most respected cancer specialists and radiation oncologists in Bangladesh.

She maintains active memberships in several prestigious international and national professional organizations, including the American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO), the Asia & Middle East Society for Therapeutic Radiology and Oncology (AMSTRO), the Bangladesh Society of Radiation Oncologists (BSRO), and the Oncology Club. These affiliations reflect her strong commitment to continuous learning, professional excellence, and advancing the field of cancer treatment in Bangladesh.

She is devoted to walking beside you in your fight against cancer — offering not just treatment, but hope, healing, and empowerment. Her extensive knowledge, cutting-edge expertise, and compassionate care provide the foundation for a positive outcome and renewed strength.
