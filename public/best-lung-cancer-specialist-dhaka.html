❓Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best lung cancer specialist in Dhaka?

Dr. Rubina Sultana is widely regarded as one of the top lung cancer specialists in Bangladesh, known for her compassionate care and expertise in radiation oncology, having over 22 years of experience.

What are common symptoms of lung cancer?

Persistent cough, chest pain, shortness of breath, unexplained weight loss, or coughing blood. Seek medical evaluation promptly.

Is radiotherapy effective for lung cancer?

Yes, modern radiotherapy improves local control and symptom relief. It can be curative in selected cases.
