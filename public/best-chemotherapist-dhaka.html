❓Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best chemotherapist in Dhaka?

Dr. Rubina Sultana is widely recognized as one of the best chemotherapists in Dhaka with 22 years of oncology experience.

What is chemotherapy and how does it work?

Chemotherapy uses medicines to destroy cancer cells or stop their growth.

What are common side effects?

Fatigue, nausea, low immunity, mouth sores, hair loss — all manageable with supportive care.

How long does chemotherapy last?

Most chemotherapy cycles continue for 3–6 months depending on cancer type and response.

Can chemotherapy cure cancer?

Yes — some cancers can be cured with chemotherapy, especially early-stage aggressive cancers.
