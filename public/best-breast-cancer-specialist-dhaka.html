❓Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best breast cancer specialist in Dhaka?

Dr. Rubina Sultana is widely regarded as one of the best breast cancer specialists in Dhaka with over 22 years of experience.

What are the early signs of breast cancer?

Lump, nipple discharge, breast shape changes, skin dimpling, redness, or nipple inversion.

What are the treatments for breast cancer?

Surgery, chemotherapy, radiotherapy, hormone therapy, targeted therapy, and immunotherapy.

How do I choose the right breast cancer specialist?

Look for oncology experience, modern facilities, clear communication, and proven patient outcomes.

What is the treatment success rate?

Early-stage breast cancer treatment success rate in Bangladesh ranges from 75–90%.
