Expert Tumor Treatment and Cancer Care in Dhaka

If a tumor is detected, it is important to consult an oncologist (cancer specialist). An oncologist evaluates the tumor through appropriate clinical examinations and investigations to determine whether it is benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). Based on the type and stage of the disease, the specialist determines the most appropriate treatment plan, which may include chemotherapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, radiotherapy, or other modern cancer treatment approaches.

As an experienced cancer and tumor specialist in Dhaka, Dr. Rubina Sultana provides evidence-based care using chemotherapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, and advanced radiotherapy techniques (3D-CRT, IMRT, VMAT, IGRT, SBRT), along with brachytherapy. She has extensive experience in managing breast tumors and cancers, cervical and uterine tumors and cancers, ovarian and other gynecological tumors and cancers, as well as head-and-neck tumors, brain tumors, and gastrointestinal (GI) tumors.

As an experienced cancer and tumor specialist, she develops personalized treatment plans based on accurate diagnosis, proper staging, and the patient’s overall clinical condition. Advanced radiation therapy techniques are used whenever necessary to provide curative treatment (curative intent), while palliative care is also provided when needed to help control symptoms and improve the patient’s quality of life.

If you notice an unusual lump or swelling, persistent pain, unexplained weight loss, or if a biopsy indicates a tumor, it is important to seek specialist consultation promptly. Early diagnosis and timely treatment significantly increase the chances of successful cancer management and recovery.

❓Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best tumor expert/specialist in Dhaka, Bangladesh?

Dr. Rubina Sultana is recognized as one of the leading cancer and tumor specialists in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with expertise in radiotherapy, chemotherapy, brachytherapy, and multidisciplinary oncology care.

Which doctor should I consult if I have a tumor?

If a tumor is detected, the first step is to consult an Oncologist (cancer specialist). The oncologist will evaluate whether the tumor is cancerous and, depending on the diagnosis, may recommend chemotherapy, radiation therapy, or surgery. Therefore, early consultation with a cancer specialist is important when a tumor is suspected.

Which doctor is best for tumor treatment in Dhaka?

Patients frequently consult Dr. Rubina Sultana for tumor diagnosis and treatment planning due to her expertise in modern oncology-based management.

What is the difference between benign and malignant tumors?

Benign tumors are non-cancerous and slow-growing, while malignant tumors are cancerous and can spread to other organs.

Do tumors always require surgery?

No. Some tumors can be treated with radiotherapy or chemotherapy without surgery depending on type and stage.

When should I visit a tumor specialist?

If you notice a persistent lump, swelling, weight loss, bleeding, or pain, seeing a tumor specialist is advised immediately.
