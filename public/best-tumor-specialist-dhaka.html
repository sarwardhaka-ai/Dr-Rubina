❓Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the best tumor specialist in Dhaka?

Dr. Rubina Sultana is widely recognized as one of Dhaka’s leading tumor and cancer specialists, particularly in the field of radiation oncology, with more than two decades of clinical experience and a strong record of evidence-based treatment planning.

What treatments are used for tumors?

Depending on type and stage: radiotherapy, chemotherapy, brachytherapy, and supportive care.

Can benign tumors turn into cancer?

Most benign tumors do not turn into cancer, but a few types may change over time. Regular monitoring with an experienced specialist is recommended.

What is the difference between benign and malignant tumors?

Benign tumors are non-cancerous and slow-growing, while malignant tumors are cancerous and can spread to other parts of the body.
