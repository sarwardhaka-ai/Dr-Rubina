About Dr. Rubina Sultana

Dr. Mosammat Rubina Sultana is a leading female cancer specialist in Dhaka with 22+ years of experience in radiation oncology, chemotherapy, and comprehensive tumor care. She is known for her compassionate communication, clinical precision, and evidence-based treatment approach.

She currently serves as an Associate Professor at the National Institute of Cancer Research & Hospital (NICRH), and consults at Ahsania Mission Cancer & General Hospital, Delta Hospital Ltd., and Enam Medical College Hospital. She provides care across breast, cervical, lung, head & neck, brain, and gastrointestinal cancers.

Her expertise includes modern technologies such as IMRT, IGRT, and SBRT, with personalized treatment plans for each patient. She is IAEA-certified and holds FCPS (Radiotherapy) and MBBS qualifications.
